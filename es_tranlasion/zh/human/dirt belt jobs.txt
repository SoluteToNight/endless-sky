mission "Drought Relief"
	name `<planet> 抗旱`
	job
	repeat
	description `<destination> 的农业世界目前正在经历干旱。由 <date> 将 <cargo> 送到地球，以供 <payment> 使用。`
	cargo "drought relief supplies" 25 2 .05
	deadline
	to offer
		random < 10
	source
		government "Republic"
		not attributes "farming"
	destination
		distance 3 10
		attributes "dirt belt"
		attributes "farming"
	on visit
		dialog phrase "generic cargo on visit"
	on complete
		payment 10000 160
		dialog "您将 <commodity> 的货物交付并收取 <payment> 的付款。 <planet> 市民向您表示感谢并向您告别。"


mission "To Remembrance Day celebration [0]"
	name `阵亡将士纪念日庆祝活动`
	job
	repeat
	description `由 <date> 将 <fare> 带到 <destination>，参加 2 月 19 日的阵亡将士纪念日庆祝活动。付款方式为<payment>。`
	passengers 4 4 .5
	deadline
	to offer
		or
			and
				random < 20
				month == 12
				day >= 21
			and
				random < 30
				month == 1
			and
				random < 10
				month == 2
				day < 19
	to fail
		month * 100 + day >= 219
	source
		attributes "dirt belt"
	destination
		distance 3 15
		attributes "dirt belt"
	on visit
		dialog phrase "generic passenger on visit"
	on complete
		payment 1000 180
		dialog "您祝您的 <passengers> 在 <planet> 一切顺利。孩子们涂着绿色颜料从你的船上跑下来，父母付给你 <payment> 。"
